Instructions:

  1. Your goal is to move the full stack of tiles to the third row down with tiles appearing in original order (descending by size to right).
  2. You will move one tile at a time. You can only move the top/outermost right tile in any row.
  3. Tiles can be be moved up or down within rows, and will be added to the top (outermost right position) of stack.
  4. Tiles can be added to empty rows or on top of larger tiles only.
  5. To begin, click on the tile you would like to move.
  6. Next, click on the destination row where you would like to move tile.
  7. Reminders:
    • You can only select and move a tile from top (outermost right position) of stack in any row.
      If you accidently select a different tile from stack, you will be prompted to select a new tile.
    • If row is not empty, tiles can only be added on top (to right) of larger tiles.
      If you are trying to move a tile that is larger than the last tile in the destination row, your tile will not be moved from original row.
